Cultural Events – Happening All Week Long

Every single day we host a small, immersive celebration from one of our five nations.
Drop in any night (or morning) you’re staying – no extra charge, just pure fun.

🇺🇸American Cultural Nights

  • Route 66 Road-Trip Evening – Classic American rock ‘n’ roll playlist, vintage car photo booth, storytelling about the open road, neon sign crafts.
  • Bluegrass Porch Jam – Live banjo & fiddle (or recorded), line-dancing lesson on the deck, campfire sing-along of Johnny Cash and Dolly Parton.

🇷🇺Russian Cultural Nights

  • Matryoshka & Fairy-Tale Workshop – Paint your own nesting doll set, listen to Baba Yaga and Firebird tales by candlelight, wear a kokoshnik headpiece for photos.
  • Balalaika & Folk Dance Night – Live (or recorded) balalaika music, easy Russian circle-dance lesson, scarf-twirling and clapping games.

🇩🇪German Cultural Nights

  • Black Forest Cuckoo Clock Evening – Build a mini cardboard cuckoo clock, Grimm brothers storytelling, yodeling contest (yes, really!).
  • Schuhplattler Dance & Lederhosen Party – Bavarian slap-dance tutorial, accordion music, pretend “beer-stein” lifting competition with root-beer mugs.

🇮🇹Italian Cultural Nights

  • Venetian Mask-Making Soirée – Decorate your own papier-mâché mask, gondola-style photo corner, mandolin serenades and Italian love songs.
  • Tarantella Dance Under the Stars – Fast-paced southern Italian folk dance lesson, tambourine playing, lantern-lit courtyard dancing.

🇫🇷French Cultural Nights

  • Provençal Lavender & Poetry Night – Make lavender sachets, read French poetry aloud (in English or French), Edith Piaf & accordion background music.
  • Can-Can & Cabaret Evening – Quick can-can dance lesson, feather-boa photos, live (or recorded) French café music and sing-along.
