The Breakfast Embassy Menu

Everyday our menu consists of two meals from each country both for breakfast and dinner — all included with your stay.

American Meals
Classic All-American Breakfast
Fluffy scrambled eggs, crispy bacon, pork sausage links, golden waffles or pancakes with real maple syrup, hash browns, and buttered toast
Biscuits & Gravy Deluxe
Two giant buttermilk biscuits smothered in creamy sausage gravy, two fried eggs, extra bacon or sausage on the side
★★★ Chicken Fried Steak Dinner
Hand-breaded beef cutlet with country gravy, mashed potatoes, green beans with bacon, and Texas toast
Cowboy Ribeye or NY Strip (6–14 oz)
Grilled to order with garlic butter, homemade mac & cheese, green beans with bacon, and a dinner roll
Russian Meals
Traditional Blini Breakfast
Thin Russian crepes with smoked salmon, red caviar, sour cream, dill, and honey or berry jam
Syrniki & Kasha
Farmer’s cheese pancakes with sour cream and jam, plus warm buckwheat porridge with butter and honey
★★★ Classic Beef Borsch Dinner
Rich beet soup with tender beef, cabbage, potatoes, and dill — served with rye bread and sour cream
Beef Stroganoff
Tender beef strips in creamy mushroom sauce over buttered egg noodles, with pickled cucumbers
German Meals
German Farmer’s Breakfast
Skillet of fried potatoes, ham, onions, and scrambled eggs, served with warm pretzel and sweet mustard
Weisswurst Frühstück
Bavarian veal sausages, soft pretzel, sweet mustard, and a breakfast radler (non-alcoholic available)
★★★ Sauerbraten Dinner
Marinated pot roast in sweet-sour gravy, red cabbage, and potato dumplings (knödel)
Schweinhaxe (Crispy Pork Knuckle)
Oven-roasted pork shank with crackling skin, sauerkraut, mashed potatoes, and dark beer gravy
Italian Meals
Cornetto & Cappuccino Breakfast
Fresh Italian croissants with jam or Nutella, perfect foamy cappuccino, and seasonal fruit
Uova in Purgatorio
Eggs poached in spicy tomato-basil sauce, crusty ciabatta, and fresh mozzarella
★★★ Osso Buco alla Milanese
Braised veal shank with saffron risotto and gremolata
Chicken or Eggplant Parmigiana
Breaded and baked with marinara and melted mozzarella, served with spaghetti aglio e olio
French Meals
★★★ Classic French Breakfast
Fresh croissants, pain au chocolat, butter & jam, café au lait, and a soft-boiled egg
Crêpes Two Ways
One savory (ham & Gruyère) and one sweet (Nutella & banana or lemon-sugar)
Coq au Vin
Chicken braised in red wine with mushrooms, pearl onions, and bacon, served with mashed potatoes
Beef Bourguignon
Tender beef slow-cooked in Burgundy wine with carrots, mushrooms, and herbs, served with crusty baguette
