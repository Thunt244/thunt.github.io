Nestled in the beautiful rolling hills and forest, The Breakfast Embassy is the world’s coziest diplomatic mission. Every morning a different country takes over the kitchen — authentic dishes made from scratch, served family-style at one big table.

Sleep in one of our eight ambassador suites (each themed after its country), wake up to the smells of fresh croissants or sizzling churros, then spend your weekend at miniature Oktoberfests, Día de los Muertos celebrations, or mooncake lantern festivals — all without ever needing a passport.
